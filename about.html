About Us

Founded on the belief that everyone deserve equitable access to real estate investments as a means of wealth creation.

Founded in 2019 on the core belief that everyone deserves equitable access to build wealth, Coreum is a first-of-its-kind online real estate co-investment platform in Nigeria.

We offer everyday investors easy access to high-quality and affordable private real estate assets.

Our process is simple: we aggregate funds from co-investors towards the purchase and/or development of strategic real estate assets with high growth potential.

Our goal is to use our experience and technological edge to lower the barriers to real estate investment in Africa by opening opportunities for individuals to co-invest in high-yield private real estate investments

Our team comprises talented individuals with diverse skill sets and varied work experience across real estate, technology, and finance with the single mission to empower the individual investor.
